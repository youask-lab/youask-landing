About

YouAsk is a is a platform developed by Asktek Innovation and Solution Private Limited. It was founded in 2025, with its headquarters located in Jaipur, India.

Our goal is to provide a digital space where people can get quick and reliable answers to their questions. By leveraging technology, we aim to make knowledge easily accessible for everyone.

On Youask, users can directly connect with companies, and experts can also earn money by offering their skills and knowledge on the platform.

To empower people with fast and trustworthy answers through a simple and intelligent platform.
