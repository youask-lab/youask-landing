    3. Cookie Policy

    At YouAsk, we are committed to being transparent about how we collect the data and use it.This cookie policy applies to any product and references that link to the services provided on the platform..

    What are cookies?

    Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit the website that help us improve your user experience.They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently. They allow us to remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, and personalize content.

    How we use cookies:

    By continuing to visit our services, you are agreeing to use the cookies and similar technologies.

    We use cookies for the following purposes:

    Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly. They enable you to navigate the platform and use its features, such as accessing secure areas. Because these cookies are essential, we may not require your separate consent for their use, but we provide you with this information about them.

    Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use the platform. such as which pages you visit most often and if you receive any error messages. This information helps us improve the platform's performance and user experience.

    Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the platform to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. They may also be used to provide services you have asked for, such as watching a video or commenting on a blog.

    Advertising/Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests. They may also be used to track the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. We may share this information with third-party advertisers..

    How to manage cookies:

    To manage cookies, you can adjust settings through both websites and browsers. Many websites provide an option to accept or reject non-essential cookies via a consent banner. You can also control cookie preferences in your browser by going to settings, where you can block third-party cookies or delete all cookies. Additionally, using Incognito or Private Browsing modes automatically limits cookie storage. Regularly clearing your browser’s cookies helps maintain privacy and ensures you're not storing unnecessary data.
