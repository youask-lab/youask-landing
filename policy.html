General Usage Policy

We, at YouAsk, are committed to ensuring the proper and fair use of our platform and services. This General Usage Policy applies to individuals who access or use the services offered on our platform, and outlines the guidelines and processes for using the platform and its features.

  • Booking window. Bookings must be made at least 6 hours prior to the scheduled session start time. If less than 6 hours remain, the session is no longer available for booking. Sessions may be booked up to 7 days in advance.
  • Joining a session. The end user may join up to 5 minutes before the scheduled start time. If the end user cannot attend or leaves early, the host must wait a minimum of 10 minutes before ending the session, or for the remaining time, whichever is shorter.
  • User cancellation. The end user may cancel up to 12 hours before the start time and receive a 90% refund. Cancellations within 12 hours are not eligible for any refund.
  • Host cancellation. The host may cancel up to 30 minutes before the start time. In such cases, the amount paid is refunded in full to the end user.
  • No-shows. If both the end user and host fail to attend, the amount paid is refunded to the end user.

If the end user is dissatisfied with the service provided, they may raise a dispute within one day of receiving the service. The internal team will review the matter and, if appropriate, issue a refund to the end user's account.

If the end user ends the session early due to an issue and raises a dispute within one day, the internal team will review the case. Based on their assessment, the end user may be refunded up to 50% of the amount paid, or a higher amount depending on the situation.

If the host is unable to provide the service as agreed, the full amount paid is refunded to the end user. In the event of technical issues, network problems, power failures, or hardware malfunctions on the host's end leading to early termination, the end user may raise a dispute within one day for a possible partial refund.

If, following a thorough investigation, the internal team determines that the host failed to provide the service with all necessary information, the host will not receive payment. The full amount is refunded and processed within 2 working days. Upon successful completion of a service, the payment is transferred to the host's account within 2 working days.

In the referral service, the end user may connect with the host and discuss relevant details, but the decision to proceed with a referral rests entirely with the host, based on their own criteria or assessment of the situation.

If you require additional information or have any concerns regarding our services, please feel free to contact us.

Cookie Policy

At YouAsk, we are committed to being transparent about how we collect and use data. This Cookie Policy applies to any product and references that link to the services provided on the platform.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website that help us improve your experience. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, and they allow us to remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, and personalize content.

How we use cookies

By continuing to visit our services, you agree to the use of cookies and similar technologies. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: necessary for the platform to function properly. They enable you to navigate and use features such as accessing secure areas.
  • Performance cookies: collect information about how you use the platform, such as which pages you visit most often, helping us improve performance.
  • Functionality cookies: remember choices you make (such as language preference) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Advertising / targeting cookies: deliver advertisements relevant to your interests and track the effectiveness of campaigns. We may share this information with third-party advertisers.
How to manage cookies

You can adjust settings through both websites and browsers. Many websites provide an option to accept or reject non-essential cookies via a consent banner. You can also control cookie preferences in your browser settings, where you can block third-party cookies or delete all cookies. Using Incognito or Private Browsing modes automatically limits cookie storage, and regularly clearing your browser's cookies helps maintain privacy.
